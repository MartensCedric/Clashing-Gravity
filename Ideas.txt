--Android Game--
Core mecanics :
On touch (anywhere on the screen)
The gravity flips, pulling the player on the other side of the screen.
The game is a pseudo sidescroller (obstacles move towards instead)
the obstacles are squares randomly spawned and going faster and faster towards the player
The games end when the player comes in contact with an obstacle

Secondary mecanics/Features :
Score is attributed by distance (time)
Players get coins with their score and can purchase other colors or textures.
Items such as 1.5 score for a limited time, slow time, a laser
coins get be collected


Other : 
Pause button in the right corner which stops score incrementing and freezes obstacles.
Button will also display options and shop (all items can be earned with in-game currency)
